Clowns have made crowds laugh for generations, but what they represent in popular culture has changed a lot over the past years. After the story of a real life killer clown, John Wayne Gacy, who murdered 33 boys shocked the country, the representation of clowns took a decidedly terrifying turn. This event redefined the clown to invoke fear and the clown image became more prominent in horror films. Later on in 2016, the influence of horror films had brought concerns from many reports of threatening looking clowns tempting to creep several citizens. Meanwhile, playing a strong presence as a dark or violent character may be popular in the media, but the attention is not always desired. Since the scary movies and incidents weighed down the image by fear and sadness, professional clowns are struggling to spread laughter.
